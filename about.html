Nice to meet you!

My passion about user centered design started when I discovered it was possible to measure the impact desing could have in people's lives.
I'm a graduated Graphic Designer and a self-taught HTML & CSS developer.

Nowadays, I'm a Product Designer & Desing Manager at Tiendanube, an ecommerce startup with more than 27.000 clients in Latin America. I enjoy being able to focus on a product and to improve our client's daily operations.

The best knowledge is shared knowledge

I strongly believe that colaboration and learning from each other are key to grow our discipline and be better professionals.
Last year, I joined Coderhouse -a digital school- as an instructor.
Ocassionaly, I speak at local events or participate to keep in touch with the design community.
I used to host my own podcast about UX for the spanish community. Check it out @uxshotpodcast.com

When I'm not in front of my computer you can find me practicing Yôga, reading a book or cooking a yummy, delicious veggie meal.
