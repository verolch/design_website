Welcome to my world of purposeful design!

My passion for user-centered design started when I realised its profound impact on people's lives. Curiosity drove me to dive deep into understanding and exploring technology, human behaviour, and everything in between. Embracing a user-centric mindset, I believe we hold the power and responsibility to create transformative experiences through design.

From powerhouse corporations like Zalando and IBM to innovative startups like Tiendanube and Honeypot, I've gathered diverse professional experiences. This exposure has strengthened my communication skills and instilled in me a deep appreciation for interdisciplinary collaboration that empowers me to deliver well-balanced, purposeful solutions.

The best knowledge is shared knowledge

I strongly believe that collaboration and learning from each other are the keys to our growth. That’s why I have always found ways to share my knowledge and learn from others. I’ve been a UX podcast host and a UX instructor. Nowadays I’m a design mentor and I'm privileged to lead the Ladies that UX community in Buenos Aires, supporting and elevating women in the design field while fostering knowledge exchange and empowerment.

Beyond design, my passion extends to the environment and the future of our planet. I'm continually exploring ways to integrate sustainability into my design practice, ensuring that my work contributes positively to the world we live in.

Recently certified as a yoga teacher, mindfulness and empathy enrich my design approach, enhancing my professional skills.

I'm thrilled by the possibilities ahead and the chance to make a difference through design. If you're interested in design, leadership, or simply want to connect I'm all ears!

Let's collaborate, learn, and build a better world together through design excellence.
