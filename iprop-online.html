Iprop Online

Real Estate Consulting & Sales

01.

THE CLIENT

Iprop is a real estate boutique company which offers their clients a professional and personalized service. They think is important to advice the client as a friend and they consider each case as a unique one. They wanted a site that would allow their clients to do their own research in a trasparent way at the same time it helped them connect with their sellers.

02.

THE CHALLENGE

Iprop needed a quick redesign of their web that was easy for their clients to use, and that would facilitate the communication between them and their prospective buyers.

03.

The Approach

We lead a quick design thinking + user research sprint and presented our findings to the client. We identified what neded to be improved on their site to better target their audience. There where a lot of sections and a lot of text on the site that nobody took the time to read. We realized the sense of confidence and efectiveness they wanted to achieve did’t have to be written on long boring paragraphs but rather shown with a easy to use, friendly platform.

04.

WIREFRAMING

With the findings of our first sprint we got started sketching on paper and innmediately got into the digital wireframes that we presented to the client.

05.

FINAL DESIGN

After reviewing the wires and the flow with our client and users we moved on to the final design.
