The Client

Atdaa

My Role

Research

Prototype - Test

Mission

Enable travelers to discover amazing experiences when visiting a new city

The challenge

As a team of people who are passionate about travelling and discovering new experiences we all use google maps to bookmark the places we love or someone has recommended to us. The problem was that as we gathered more stars in our map they start to clutch up and suddenly you have a lot and you start forgetting why you bookmarked a place, who recommended it to you and what was so appealing that you decided to save it.

The goal

We decided to work on an app that will let you quickly recognize why you marked down a place and filter the kind of information you are looking for. Also it would let you follow the people whose taste you trust for specific things.

Competitive Analysis

Before jumping into the design we analized which companies on the market where closest to solving our problem and why they where failing according to our expectations.

Hard to trust the info because you don’t know the users and maybe your taste does not match theirs

A user generated travel content platform with lack of good design direction and trust.

It’s difficult to tell what the bookmarks mean and the results are too general.

User Research

We explored the market, conducted casual interviews with our target users and created user personas and journeys to have a better understanding of our potential users needs and the impact on what we were building.

App Design

The central point for our work in designing the app was simplifying the experience of finding places near you on-the-go. We always kept in mind the use cases and wondered what was important for the users.

Visual elements

We finally got started on the visual aspects of our design. We set a color palette, an icon set and map style.

Color Palette

We decided to create a colorfull but soft color palette to go with Atdaa’s bright orange.
